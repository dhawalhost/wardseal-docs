# Access Reviews & Certifications

Automate the process of auditing user access to sensitive resources and applications.

## Why Access Reviews?

Security and compliance standards (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA) require organizations to periodically certify that users have only the access they need. WardSeal automates this to eliminate manual spreadsheets.

## Creating a Campaign

1. **Scope**: Choose the applications or groups to review.
2. **Reviewers**: Assign managers or application owners to perform the review.
3. **Timeline**: Set a start date and a deadline (e.g., 30 days).
4. **Frequency**: Configure recurring reviews (e.g., quarterly or annually).

## The Review Process

Reviewers receive a dashboard listing all users with access to the scoped resources. For each user, they must choose:
- **Approve**: User keeps their access.
- **Revoke**: Access is automatically removed once the campaign finishes.
- **Abstain**: Delegate the decision to another reviewer.

## Audit Logs & Reporting

WardSeal generates a tamper-evident PDF report at the end of every campaign, documenting every decision made, who made it, and when.
