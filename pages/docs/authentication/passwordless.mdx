# Passwordless Authentication

Reduce friction and improve security by eliminating passwords altogether.

## Passkeys (WebAuthn)

Passkeys are the gold standard for passwordless authentication. They use public-key cryptography and device-level biometrics (like TouchID/FaceID) to provide a phishing-resistant login experience.

### Benefits
- **Phishing Resistant**: Unlike passwords or SMS codes, passkeys cannot be intercepted or stolen.
- **Fast**: Users log in with a single tap or look.
- **Secure**: No secrets are stored on the WardSeal servers.

## Magic Links

Users enter their email address and receive a unique, short-lived link that logs them in directly.

### Best Practices
- Use Magic Links for initial onboarding.
- Encourage users to register a Passkey as their primary authentication factor.
