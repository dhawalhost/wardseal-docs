# Policy: Incident Response and Breach Notification Policy

**Effective Date:** 15 March 2026

This policy outlines how WardSeal handles security incidents and personal data breach notifications.

## 1. Incident lifecycle

1. Detection and triage
2. Containment and eradication
3. Recovery and validation
4. Post-incident review and corrective actions

## 2. Severity framework

- **SEV-1:** Active compromise or major service impact
- **SEV-2:** High-risk issue with limited blast radius
- **SEV-3:** Moderate issue requiring planned remediation

## 3. Notification commitments

When required by law or contract, WardSeal will notify affected customers without undue delay after confirming a reportable incident.

Notification includes, where available:

- Nature of incident
- Categories of affected data
- Likely impact
- Mitigation actions taken
- Recommended customer actions

## 4. Communication channels

- Primary: security@wardseal.com
- Secondary support channel: https://help.wardseal.com

## 5. Customer obligations

Customers should promptly report suspected incidents, maintain secure integrations, and cooperate with containment actions.

## 6. Post-incident actions

WardSeal documents root cause, lessons learned, and preventive measures, and updates runbooks and controls as needed.
