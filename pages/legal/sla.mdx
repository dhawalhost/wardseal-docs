# Policy: Service Level Policy (SLA)

**Effective Date:** 15 March 2026

This SLA defines service availability and support targets for WardSeal cloud services.

## 1. Availability target

- Monthly uptime target: 99.9%
- Measurement scope: production service endpoints only
- Exclusions: scheduled maintenance, customer-caused issues, force majeure, internet backbone/provider outages beyond WardSeal control

## 2. Support response targets

| Severity | Example | Initial Response Target |
|---|---|---|
| Sev 1 | service outage/security-critical issue | 1 hour |
| Sev 2 | major functionality impaired | 4 hours |
| Sev 3 | moderate issue/workaround available | 1 business day |
| Sev 4 | minor issue/question | 2 business days |

## 3. Maintenance windows

Planned maintenance windows and customer notice periods are published at https://help.wardseal.com or communicated directly.

## 4. Service credits

If monthly uptime falls below target, eligible customers may request service credits as defined in commercial terms.

## 5. Customer responsibilities

Customers must maintain supported configurations, secure integrations, and timely incident reporting.

## 6. Enterprise/self-hosted

Self-hosted deployments are outside this cloud SLA unless separately contracted.
