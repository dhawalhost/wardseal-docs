# Policy: Subprocessors and International Transfers

**Effective Date:** 15 March 2026

This document lists categories of subprocessors used to deliver WardSeal services and describes transfer safeguards.

## 1. Subprocessor categories

| Category | Purpose | Data Types | Typical Region |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cloud hosting provider | compute/network/storage | service data and metadata | India / EU / US (as contracted) |
| Managed database provider | relational data storage | identity and configuration data | India / EU / US (as contracted) |
| Observability/logging provider | monitoring and troubleshooting | logs and telemetry | India / EU / US (as contracted) |
| Support platform | ticketing and customer support | account/support communications | India / EU / US (as contracted) |
| Email/notification service | transactional communications | contact and notification metadata | India / EU / US (as contracted) |

## 2. Subprocessor change process

- Maintain current list at this document location
- Provide notice period of 15 days for material changes when required by agreement
- Provide objection process where contractually required

## 3. International transfers

Where personal data moves across jurisdictions, WardSeal applies lawful transfer mechanisms such as SCCs and relevant supplementary measures.
