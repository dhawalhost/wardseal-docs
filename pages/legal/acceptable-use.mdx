# Policy: Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** 15 March 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) governs use of WardSeal services.

You must not use WardSeal to:

- Violate laws, regulations, or third-party rights
- Distribute malware or conduct phishing, credential stuffing, or denial-of-service attacks
- Attempt unauthorized access, privilege escalation, or tenant boundary bypass
- Interfere with service operation, security controls, or monitoring
- Process unlawful content or data categories prohibited by agreement
- Reverse engineer or abuse APIs beyond documented limits

## Enforcement

WardSeal may investigate suspected violations and may suspend or terminate access for confirmed violations, security risks, or legal requirements.

## Reporting abuse

Report suspected abuse to: security@wardseal.com
